Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Marceline, operated by Rotten Network ("we", "us"), collects and uses information when you interact with our Discord application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Discord user data: user IDs, usernames, avatar URLs.
  • Server data: server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and configuration settings.
  • Command and interaction data: content necessary to process commands or features.
  • Log data: timestamps, error logs, and usage statistics for debugging and performance.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate and provide Marceline’s features.
  • To store server-specific settings and preferences.
  • To improve stability, performance, and security.
  • To prevent abuse or misuse of the App.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We store only the minimum data required for the App to function. Configuration data may remain stored while the App is installed on a server. We do not store full message histories.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We may share limited data only when:

  • Working with hosting or infrastructure providers.
  • Required by law or legal process.
  • Necessary to protect users or the platform.

5. Third‑Party Services

Marceline operates on Discord’s platform. Discord’s own policies govern your use of their service. Rotten Network does not control Discord’s data handling.

6. Children’s Privacy

Marceline is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored data, but no system is completely secure.

8. Your Rights

Server owners may remove the App at any time. You may request deletion of specific data by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@rotten.network.
